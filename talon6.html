The Talon6

A marvel of Technology

  • Quality - Made with no expense spared, quality workmanship, and extensive QC
  • Endurance - 40 minutes of quiet flight time for extended missions
  • Heavylift - 1-4kg reccomended max payload, no need to worry about adding weight

Made in Canada for the Wilderness

Rugged Robust Construction makes this drone waterproof, and capable of operating at high altitude, in subzero, or the desert heat - Battery rail and clip design for fast and secure battery swapping. Top and Bottom payload carriages enable versatile mission parameters.

Opensource

The Talon6 was designed using opensource hardware and software, allowing you to customize it to your needs. The Talon6 uses the renowned Pixhawk flight controller to run APM Copter software, giving users the ability to integrate companion computers as well as various sensing and operational hardware. An onboard variable voltage power supply enables you to easily plug in whatever equipment you need. In short the Talon6 is ready for your custom development needs.

Specifications

  • Weight - 6.6-9.2 kg (depending on battery config)
  • Reccomended Payload - 1-4kg
  • Reccomended Maximum Take-off weight (MTOW) - 10kg
  • Rate of Climb - 10m/s
  • Max Translational Speed - 15m/s ~ 50km/h
  • Dimensions - 1.64m (Wingspan) 1.0m (motor to motor)
  • Max Hover Time - 40 mins, 35 mins with DSLR (1kg payload)
  • Battery Capacity - 444Wh - 20Ah, 22.2V, 6S2P configuration or 40Ah6s
  • Motors - 87-30 Brushless DC Outrunners
  • Propellers - CFD optimized : 660mm Diameter
  • Waterproofing - IP43
  • Flight range - 1km, up to 50km with UHF add on
  • Construction - Carbon Fiber, Titanium, Aluminum, PLA, Silicon wiring
  • GPS Positional Accuracy - 2m, down to cm precision with RTK GPS add on
  • Waypoing navigation - grid survey capable, computer operable (no transmitter required)
  • Sound - Turbulence reduction propeller configuration
